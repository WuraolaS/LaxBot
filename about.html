About The Meditation Lax Bot

The LAX Meditation Bot is a Chat Bot that creates specific meditation exercises for users based on their mood or emotion. Through a simple conversation between the user and the Chat Bot a meditation style and length is created specifically for the emotion the user expresses. The main purpose of the LAX Bot is to create a safe and judgement free environment for the users to express themselves in hopes of finding the right meditation that fits their current emotion.   Check out Lax Bot and learn what meditation exercise you should do!
